Welcome!

Welcome to The Dice Goblins Dungeon. I am Dominic, a Professional Dungeon Master. I run online D&D sessions for individual players and groups primarily based in my own homebrew worlds. I am passionate about D&D and love to create magical worlds for others to explore. I love introducing new players to the world of D&D so don't let a lack of experience hold you back. I offer extensive support to new players so they can feel confident exploring this fantastical world.

What is D&D?

For anyone unfamiliar with D&D, it is a table top roleplaying game involving both players and a dungeon master or DM. The DM uses various tools to illustrate the world, giving descriptions and images to set the scene. They weave the story for the players, explaining what is happening in the world to engage the players. Players decide what actions they wish to take and the DM will narrate the outcomes of these choices. The DM controls the other characters in the worlds whilst players generally have one character. It is a collaborative story telling game with a mixture of combat and roleplaying.

Example:
DM: You walk into a tavern, it is crowded with people as they hurry inside to escape the bitter cold outside. Everyone seems to be swept up in the atmosphere except one; a hooded figure in the corner of the room. What do you do?
Player: I stand at the bar, keeping one eye on the figure. Can I get a feel for this individual, maybe pick up on their mood?
DM: Roll insight for me.
Player: (rolls dice) I rolled 15. With my bonus that is a total of 19.
DM: Great! You notice that the figure is a woman, she appears to use the hood to hide her face, her eyes dart around the room. She seems to be looking for someone, or something.
